About us

OUR MISSION
"Make India hunger free"
We are India's 100% transparent online donation platform for charities, where people can directly donate food needed instead of money. Our vision is to bring more transparency to online crowdfunding so that we provide an opportunity for every Indian & NRI to give back to society confidently.
